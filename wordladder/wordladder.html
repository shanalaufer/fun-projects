How to play

    

      
Climb from the Start word to the
        Goal word. Each step, type a new word that:

      
    
        
  • is the same length,
    • 
        
  • changes exactly one letter, and
    • 
        
  • keeps every other letter in its exact position.
    • 
      


      

        

          ✓ valid
          RIGHT → LIGHT
          only position 1 changed (R→L)
        

        

          ✗ not valid
          RIGHT → TIGER
          letters rearranged / several positions changed
        

      


      
Par is the shortest possible
        solution — your target to match. Steps counts your moves.

      
No timer pressure and no way to lose — solve at your own pace.
        Stuck? Hint reveals a good next word; Solution shows one full ladder.

      
e.g. COLD → CORD → CARD → WARD → WARM
